About LoveCat Rescue

Founded in 2020, LoveCat Rescue is a volunteer-run non-profit organization based in Beijing, China.

We follow the global TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) standard to humanely control stray cat populations while providing medical care, sterilization, and adoption services.

1,200+ cats rescued | 800+ successfully adopted

  • Officially registered non-profit
  • All cats are spayed/neutered, vaccinated & dewormed
  • 100% transparent financial reports

Our Mission

Rescue

Respond to reports of injured, sick, or abandoned cats and provide immediate shelter and care.

Rehabilitate

Partner with veterinarians to offer spay/neuter, vaccinations, and treatment for illnesses.

Rehome

Match healthy cats with loving, responsible forever families with lifelong follow-up.
